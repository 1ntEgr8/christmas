So Santa, what's the deal?

As usual, I want to make Christmas as joyous and cheerful as can be. This time I decided to embrace tech and use it to spread the joy of Christmas. So I teamed up with 1ntEgr8 to build this tool!

But, what exactly does this tool do?

Basically, it creates a unique, yes UNIQUE, pixelated image of a christmas tree with a Christmas greeting that contains your name and your recepient's name. Kinda like the pic in the card. You can use it to submit a Christmas greeting to at most 10 people! and yes, it's FREE (no ads :))

What's so special about it?

  • It's much better than a plain old Merry Christmas text message
  • It super fast and easy to use
  • The algorithm ensures that your greeting cards are not only UNIQUE but that each recepient receives a pattern that is based on a UNIQUE pattern created just for you, thereby giving an added personal touch to your greetings!
